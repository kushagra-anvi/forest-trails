Overview

Forest Trails Township, Curated For Distinct Ways Of Living

Paranjape Forest Trails Bhugaon is not just apartments — it is the township environment lifestyle. Most Pune projects sell buildings; Forest Trails sells an ecosystem built around greenery, open spaces, wellness, and nature-centric living. The township spans approximately 170–190 acres. The township promotes large green zones, thousands of planted trees, valley views, equestrian facilities, sports infrastructure, and a low-density environment within a large integrated development.
